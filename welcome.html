El Niño 2026 & Guanacaste — Forecast Summary
IMN has elevated its status to Warning Stage: El Niño's arrival is now considered certain. Guanacaste faces rainfall deficits of up to 50% in H2 2026 — the highest projected for any Costa Rican province. The event is expected to extend through H2 2026 and into early 2027. This extract covers the official IMN forecast and duration & intensity assessments (Sections 1–2). Sources: IMN, NOAA, La Nación, El Observador, CR Hoy.

Water Service & Irrigation — Important Notice
Water service for both the villas and common areas has been provided through Gestión El Oasis, administered by Hotel Villas del Sueño, since construction. Irrigation restrictions have always been in place as a result. The authorized irrigation schedule is 6:00 a.m. to 10:00 a.m. only. Since March 1, 2025, there have been six temporary suspensions of up to 1–4 days due to leak repairs, well maintenance, or power outages — all handled as required. Please note that due to current heat and high temperatures, irrigated areas may appear dry later in the day despite being watered during authorized hours. This is a normal effect of heat and evaporation. Any concerns about common areas should be submitted formally by email to exibel2025@hotmail.com, copying the Oasis Committee Members.
